I am a fourth year PhD student in the Functional Programming Lab at the University of Nottingham, under the supervision of Prof. Thorsten Altenkirch.

Currently, I am in the final year of my PhD, wrapping up my research. I am on the job market and am interested in postdoctoral research positions starting from October 2025.

Previously, I obtained a BSc (Hons) Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Malta, and an MSc Computer Science from the University of Nottingham.

Broadly, my research interests lie in logic, homotopy type theory, functional programming, and constructive mathematics.
